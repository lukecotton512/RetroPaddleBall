RetroPaddleBall is a game where you try to keep a ball from hitting the bottom of the screen for as long as you can. The ball speeds up gradually as you go along. 


Power-Ups


There are two power-ups, one that speeds up the ball and gives you a speed multiplier of 3x, and another one that slows down the ball and gives you a speed multiplier of 2x. 


Score


You get 20 points for hitting the paddle with the ball, and 10 points for hitting the walls, times the score multiplier. Also, at a random time, one of the walls turns red, allowing you to get 250 points each time you hit the wall times the score multiplier. For example, if you got the speed up power-up and you hit the red wall, you can get up to 750 points for hitting the wall. 


Other


The transparent part of the blue walls is where the ball can go, but the paddle can't, to prevent squishing of the ball.
